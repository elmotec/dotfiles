# Munger Mental Models & Worldly Wisdom

A Claude skill that applies Charlie Munger's complete decision-making framework — distilled from *Poor Charlie's Almanack* — to investment analysis, bias detection, and complex decisions. It gives Claude the latticework of mental models, the 10-principle investing checklist, and Munger's 25 psychological tendencies of human misjudgment.

## What It Does

- **Investment analysis**: Runs any stock or business through Munger's full checklist — circle of competence, inversion, 10 investing principles, psychological bias check — and delivers a structured verdict
- **Bias diagnosis**: Identifies which of the 25 psychological tendencies are active in a situation and flags dangerous Lollapalooza combinations (multiple biases reinforcing each other)
- **Multidisciplinary thinking**: Applies big ideas from mathematics, physics, biology, psychology, economics, and history to any complex decision
- **Inversion**: Leads every analysis by asking what causes failure before assessing upside

## When to Use It

- "Analyze this investment like Munger would"
- "What mental models apply to this situation?"
- "Spot the psychological biases in this decision"
- "Evaluate this business using worldly wisdom"
- "Run the Munger checklist on this"
- "What would Munger say about X?"
- "Explain why this market bubble / corporate scandal happened"
